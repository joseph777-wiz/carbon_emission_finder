Eco-Tips for a Greener Campus

  • Turn off electrical loads in classrooms and labs when not in use.
  • Switch to LED lighting in corridors and hostels to lower demand immediately.
  • Unplug chargers and equipment in labs when they are not charging.
  • Use natural light in the library and open areas whenever possible.
  • Opt for college bus or carpooling to reduce emissions from personal vehicles.
